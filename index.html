History of AGRUPA athletics club

The Agrupació Atlètica Catalunya is an athletics club founded in 1981 by a group of managers, coaches and athletes.

It is in Barcelona; General and Technical Secretariats at the Estadi J. Serrahima with presence on the CEM Can Dragó court.

AGRUPA, named as it is familiarly known, organizes teams to all the athletic categories from Sub10 to seniors and practices all the athletic specialties. The minor categories (from Sub6 to Sub14) are framed in the Fundació AA Catalunya, created with the aim of spreading this sport.

History of LÔ ESPORT

Lô Esport was established as an athletics club on August 4, 2011 in the town of Alaior by a group of young athletes from the municipality.

At the moment Lô Esport, after ten years of activity, has 5 sports sections in operation and exceeds 200 athletes, with athletics schools in Alaior, Mercadal, Maó and Cala en Porter. Its main efforts are based on the training of its athletes, transmitting the values of sport to society and giving facilities to all athletes in preparing their goals. In 2018, the Esport, Salut & Familia program was born to reconcile the sports life of families with school-age children, offering training to the mothers and fathers of the children of our athletics schools at the same time as their children. The women's initiation program was also born in 2018 to integrate women into the world of sport.
